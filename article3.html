Lets play it. Below the image of the first app's screen you can find a botton to start the game.

First read how it works.
On the second screen you should pick the desire period of your game activity. For examlpe 10seconds or sixty seconds. Just click your chosen button.

To avoid the case when you are not ready to start the third screen helps. Click the button shown on the image below if you want to start playing.

Click on the circle mark as fastest as you can, but be aware sizing and color of the mark will be changed every your click.

Your score will be able for seen when the time counter countdown to zero. Then you will get to the next screen below. There you could find your result. By the way the button to play again is also there over the board. Good luck!
